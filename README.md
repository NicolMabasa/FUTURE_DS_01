
Business Sales Performance Analytics

About the Task : 
 Analyze business sales data to identify revenue trends, top-selling products, high-value categories, and regional performance.

Dataset Used : 
  Superstore Sales Dataset
  https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/vivek468/superstore-dataset-final

Tools Used :
  Power BI


Key Insights : 
  Sales Trend: Overall sales showed a consistent upward trend from 2014 to 2017, with a significant spike in 2017.
  Regional Performance: The West region recorded the highest sales while the South recorded the   lowest, indicating an opportunity to investigate and address underperformance in the South.
  Category Performance: Technology is the most profitable category while Furniture, despite reasonable sales, generates very low profit due to excessive discounting.
  Product Performance: Technology products dominate the top 10 best sellers, reinforcing Technology as the strongest performing category across the business.
